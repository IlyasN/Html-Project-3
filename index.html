History In the Making

The Nba finals have been nothing but amazing. But at the end the Golden State Warriors won. They deserved it after one of the best seasons in franchise history. They
made history by becoming the 9th team to win 67 games in a season. The others are ...

  • 1995-96 Bulls
  • 1971-72 Lakers
  • 1996-97 Bulls
  • 1996-67 76ers
  • 1972-73 Celtics
  • 1985-86 Celtics
  • 1991-92 Bulls
  • 2006-07 Mavericks
  • 2014-15 Warriors
